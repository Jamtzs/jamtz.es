The Project

SPV V.2

It is an UI upgrade for SPV. it is a cloud technology-based application developed to help investors, policymakers and individual consumers identify suitable areas for solar photovoltaic (PV) panel installation or plant construction.

Duration: 3 Months.
Methods: Surveys, Interviews, Card Sort, Preference Test, Wireframes, Prototyping.
Tools: Figma, Zeplin, Hotjar, Google analytics.
Click to see the version SPV V.1
Context

What’s the primary aim of SPV ?

It is to provide quick and easy access to a short-term PV energy production forecast anywhere in the world, at the click of a button and entering a few key parameters. Locations and PV plant information can save, downloaded and uploaded for future consultation.

The challenger

User experience in the third world

While doing research on various platforms, we found they mostly provide a similar user interface and user experience solutions but, usually the user needs good quality devices.

We found that depending on which potential countries could use the software, the power plant's energy model or other information based on the time. The software must have to adapt to all devices in the market; principally the low cost and old versions.
