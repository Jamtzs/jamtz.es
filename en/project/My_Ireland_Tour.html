Surprising moments, epic adventures and all that can happen... are waiting for you!
The Project

The My Ireland Tour website is an Ireland-based e-commerce redesign proposal that aims to improve the booking system through user-driven development.

Duration: 2 Months.

Role: UX/UI Designer, Team Leader.

Methods: User personas, Customer flow, Research, Wireframing, UI Design, Low and Mid - fidelity prototypes.

Tools: Figma, Asana.
Context

What's "My Ireland Tour"?

My Ireland Tour is an independent family business based in Cork. They offer a range of Ireland tour packages as well as their bespoke private tours. where they show you what makes Ireland such a magical place.

The challenger

Design a responsive e-commerce website that helps the user/customer to book a travel guide to move around the island, set the route and the places he wants to visit.

In addition, the website needs other components to make a better experience, such as: the newsletter section, establish a new information architecture and an administration section (back-office) where the customer can book all the activities of the season or special promotions that have been designed based on their tastes or specifications previously chosen.

PROTOTYPE

High fidelity

Design Changes.
We made changes based on feedback from prototype users and other designers. We created a newer, high-fidelity version of the platform that featured more whitespace, better interactions with components, rearranged page sections based on business goals, and clearer text.

For this design, I applied Gestalt Principles, colour theory, and used grids to ensure consistency throughout the application.

Design System Language.

We made changes based on the user feedback from the prototype and anothers designers. We created a newer, high-fidelity version of the app that featured more white space, larger icons and clearer text.

To this design, I applied the Gestalt Principles, colour theory and made use of grids to ensure consistency within the entire app.
